    • FAQ

    Where can I find the prices?

    Our prices vary from one localization to another.

    If you need a detailed price list, please contact us via email.

    Can I order a delivery from The Bakery?

    None of our bakeries offers delivery.

    Nonetheless, we will be happy to greet you in one of our locals!

    I have sent you an email. How long will it take for you to respond?

    Firstly, thank you for your email!

    Our Team is constantly working on replying to our customers' queries as soon as possible. However, due to the volume of the emails we receive, it may take us up to 48 hours to respond.

    How can I place an order for a cake?

    If you want to place an order for a cake or any other product, please contact us via email.

    What is more, please also describe as many details of the order as you can: quantity, type, taste, toppings etc.

    What if I need to cancel my order?

    Any order can be cancelled for 48 hours after receiving a confirmation from our Team.

    Please contact us via email or call.

    What if don't like what I purchased?

    We ask our customer to be thoughtful while placing an order as we do not offer any types of refunds and purchased goods cannot be returned.

    What makes your products different?

    We aim to achieve the best possible quality and incredible taste with every single one of our products.

    We also opt for ingredients delivered by nearby producers and farmers in order to support local businesses.
